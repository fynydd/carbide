Short Codes

The following are short codes (bits of text) you can enter into most any text input box to display data like company name.

Company Info

You can insert company info from your site settings. These snippets of text use the format {{fieldName}} where "fieldName" is the name of the field in camel case format. For example, to insert the company street address, type:

{{companyStreetAddress}}

You can find field names by simply hovering your mouse over a caption in the left column. A tooltip will appear showing you the field name.
